Chinese Pork Dumplings

Description

Jiaozi. One of the most ubiquitous types of Chinese dumplings, jiaozi (饺子) are usually steamed or boiled and have been enjoyed across China for millennia. They often have a mixture of minced pork, shrimp, vegetables, mushrooms, and aromatics, along with a paper-thin dumpling wrapper.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon seasoned rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon finely chopped Chinese chives
  • 1 tablespoon sesame seeds
  • 1 teaspoon chile-garlic sauce (such as Sriracha)

Steps

  1. Prepare dipping sauce
  2. Prepare dumplings
  3. Place a dumpling wrapper
  4. Wet the edge with little water and climp together
  5. Heat 1 to 2 tablespoons vegetable oil and place 8 to 10 dumplings on the pan.
  6. Pour in 1 cup of water; cover and cook until the dumplings are tender
  7. Repeat to cook remaining dumpings
  8. Serve dumplings with dipping sauce
